Street Food Trivia

Kwek-Kwek
Ingredients: Quail egg, batter, oil, salt.
History: Popular Filipino street snack from the 1980s. Crispy orange batter, best served with vinegar.
Fishball
Ingredients: Fish paste, flour, oil.
History: Iconic street snack, affordable and easy to eat. Usually dipped in sweet or spicy sauce.
Isaw
Ingredients: Chicken intestine, vinegar, oil.
History: Traditional Filipino street food. Grilled and served with spicy vinegar dip.
Kikiam
Ingredients: Ground pork, shrimp, bean thread, flour, spices.
History: Street snack adapted from Chinese cuisine. Usually fried and served with sweet sauce.
Betamax
Ingredients: Coagulated chicken blood, skewers, oil, spices.
History: Named because of its square shape, popular in the streets. Grilled and dipped in spicy sauce.
Palamig
Ingredients: Jelly, sugar, water, flavorings.
History: Refreshing cold drink, perfect for hot days. Comes in chocolate, pandan, gulaman, cucumber flavors.
